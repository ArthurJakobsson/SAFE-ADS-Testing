Analyze car crash data from crash sketches and crash summaries to extract detailed information about Actors, the Road Network, and Environmental Conditions. The crash occurred on a merging road, and you will provide structured data within specific fields. A Merging Road is a road that gradually joins another road, requiring vehicles to adjust their speed and position to safely integrate into the traffic flow. In a merging scenario, the two roads are typically referred to as:

**Main Road** – The primary road with continuous traffic flow, where merging vehicles must yield.
**On-ramp** – The road or lane from which vehicles enter and blend into the main road's traffic.

Extract the following details:

- **Actors**: Identify each vehicle involved and document the following:
  - **Model**: Model of the car, always choose from [Sedan, SUV, Minivan, Pickup, Semi Truck].
  - **Initial_position**: Initial position of the car, always choose the starting position from [Main road, On-ramp].
  - **Actions**: Always choose the actions from [Move forward, Merge, Turn left, Turn right].
  - **Speed**: Record the speed in mph. You may find speed limits from sketch.

- **Road network**:
  - **Number of lanes**: Provide the total number of lanes on the main road.

- **Env (Environment)**:
  - **Time**: Specify whether it was Daytime or Nighttime.
  - **Weather**: Always choose from [Sunny, Cloudy, Overcast, Rainy, Snowy, Foggy, Windy, Clear].

# Output Format

The output should be structured in JSON format, clearly separating information into the defined categories: Actors, Road network, and Env. Each category should contain the specified fields and details extracted from the data.

# Examples

## Example 1
**Input**:
Crash summary - "A 2022 BMW 320i(Vehicle 1) was driving from west to east on the main road and was about to pass the merging road. At this time, a semi truck(Vehicle 2) was merging into the main road from the on-ramp. Due to darkness and rain, the vision of both parties was unclear, resulting in a collision between the two vehicles. The main road is a two-way four-lanes freeway."
Crash sketch - A sketch of the accident road and the vehicle's trajectory, showing the direction of North and the speed limit of 70 mph for all types of cars.
**Output**:
```json
{
  "Actors": [
    {
      "Vehicle_1": {
        "Model": "Sedan",
        "Initial_position": "Main road",
        "Actions": "Move forward",
        "Speed": "70"
      },
      "Vehicle_2": {
        "Model": "Semi Truck",
        "Initial_position": "On-ramp",
        "Actions": "Merge",

      }

  ],
  "Road network": {
    "Number of lanes": "4"

```

# Notes

- If specific details are not mentioned in the crash summary, use "N/A" for unavailable values.
- Pay close attention to keywords in the text to accurately extract and categorize information.
- The structure of the extracted output must strictly follow the JSON format specified above, maintaining clarity and consistency.
